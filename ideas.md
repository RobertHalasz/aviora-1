## Idea 1 — "Dark Mineral Editorial" (Probability: 0.07)

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Institutional / Brutalist Editorial
**Core Principles:**
- Near-black warm charcoal base (#0F0E0C) with off-white text
- Extreme typographic hierarchy: massive display serif vs. tight sans body
- Asymmetric section layouts with deliberate tension
- Bronze (#A07040) as the sole accent — used only for CTAs, dividers, and active states

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black creates the sense of a private vault. Bronze is the only warmth — it signals value without shouting. White text feels like engraved letterpress on dark stone.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed dark sections with wide left-margin text columns. Hero is split 55/45 with text left-dominant. Services use a staggered asymmetric grid (1 large + 7 smaller). Sections separated by thin bronze hairlines.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin bronze horizontal rules as section dividers
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02...) in muted bronze, barely visible
- Subtle grid-dot texture overlaid on dark sections at 3% opacity

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals — text links underline with a slow bronze sweep. Cards lift with a 2px shadow shift. Buttons scale 0.97 on press.

**Animation:** Fade-up entrance (translateY 24px → 0, opacity 0 → 1, 600ms ease-out) staggered per section. Parallax on hero image at 0.3 speed ratio.

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond (Italic, 700) — aristocratic, editorial
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean, modern, readable
- Labels/Caps: DM Sans (600, letter-spacing 0.15em, uppercase)

## Idea 2 — "Warm Mineral Light" (Probability: 0.06) ← SELECTED

**Design Movement:** Quiet Luxury / Private Advisory Office
**Core Principles:**
- Warm off-white/cream base (#F5F2EE) with deep charcoal text
- Cobalt blue (from logo) used sparingly for structural accents and the founder section
- Matte bronze (#9A7B4F) for all CTAs, hover states, and micro-accents
- Generous whitespace — sections breathe like a premium print magazine

**Color Philosophy:** Cream base evokes high-quality paper stock. The cobalt blue anchors brand identity without dominating. Bronze is the action color — warm, understated, expensive. Together they feel like a private wealth management firm's annual report.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-weighted editorial layout. Hero: full-width with text left, architectural image right. Services: asymmetric — one featured large card + 7 supporting. Markets: dark cobalt contrast section. Founder: split with credentials sidebar. Network: elegant wordmark wall.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin bronze vertical rule on left of major text blocks
- Rectangular frames (echoing the logo) used as image containers
- Section labels in small caps with wide tracking

**Interaction Philosophy:** Understated hover effects — bronze underline sweep, subtle card border glow. No flashy transforms. Everything feels considered and deliberate.

**Animation:** Gentle fade-up (translateY 20px, opacity 0→1, 500ms) on scroll entry. Hero text staggers by 100ms per line. Parallax on hero image (0.2 ratio). Marquee for network section at very slow speed.

**Typography System:**
- Display Headlines: Cormorant Garamond (Italic 600/700) — refined, editorial authority
- Subheadings: Cormorant Garamond (Regular 400) — elegant contrast
- Body: Inter (400/500) — clean, highly readable
- Labels: Inter (600, uppercase, 0.12em tracking)

## Idea 3 — "Deep Cobalt Institutional" (Probability: 0.05)

**Design Movement:** Corporate Luxury / Investment Bank Aesthetic
**Core Principles:**
- Deep cobalt (#2B4A8A) as the primary background for hero and key sections
- White and light cream for text, bronze for accents
- Structured grid with generous padding — feels like a Bloomberg terminal meets luxury brand

**Color Philosophy:** Cobalt directly mirrors the logo, creating total brand coherence. The depth of the blue signals seriousness and institutional credibility. Bronze punctuates with warmth.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width cobalt hero with centered text. Alternating light/dark sections. Services in a structured 3-column grid on white. Markets in a dark cobalt panel with location tags.

**Signature Elements:**
- Cobalt-to-deep-navy gradient backgrounds
- White geometric line accents (thin rectangles, echoing logo frame)
- Bronze dot separators between credential items

**Interaction Philosophy:** Clean, professional hover states. Buttons with bronze border on hover. Nav links with bronze underline.

**Animation:** Slide-in from left for text, fade-in for images. 400ms transitions throughout.

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Playfair Display (Bold) — classic luxury
- Body: Source Sans Pro (Regular) — clean corporate
- Labels: Source Sans Pro (Semibold, uppercase)
